Sonoma County Professional Chapter

Mission and Vision

Engineers Without Borders - Sonoma County Professional Chapter (EWB-SP) supports community-driven development programs worldwide through the design and implementation of sustainable engineering projects, while fostering responsible leadership.

Our vision is a world in which all communities have the capacity to meet their basic human needs.
